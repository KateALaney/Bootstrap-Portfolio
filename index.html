About Me

I am a brand-new web developer with very little experience in web development, but who is eager to learn and is especially excited to put that knowledge to practical use. My background is in education, where I taught at the collegiate level, and curriculum development for various industries, such as the railroad. I have also worked as a curriculum developer in conjunction with the Department of Justice's Office of Community Policing Services, and was a part of the team responsible for the redesign and implementation of the COPS Office's elearning website. Both education and curriculum development required the utilization of current learning technologies, such as Articulate's Storyline and Rise, as well as Adobe's Dreamweaver, Illustrator, and InDesign, and I am looking forward to adding the MERN stack to my portfolio of technologies.

I have a unique viewpoint as a result of my background in education and curriculum development, along with the technologies and skills I've developed and used, blended with those I'm currently learning, and I believe I am well-positioned to be a useful member of any production team.
